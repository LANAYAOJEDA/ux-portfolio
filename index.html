Hola! My name is Lizeth, and I am a digital content strategist with a passion for user experience (UX). As a content strategist, my primary goal is to create content that connects with users and helps them achieve their goals. Whether I am developing a content strategy for a website or social media platform. I always keep the user at the forefront of my mind. I believe that content and UX go hand-in-hand. By understanding how users interact with digital interfaces, I can create content that is both engaging and effective. I am skilled in conducting user research, creating user personas, and developing content that meets the needs of different audience segments. I am committed to continuous learning and growth, and I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and stay ahead of the curve.
